Welcome to Hush

One-click silence for your Mac notifications

Setting up automatically…

Hush needs two shortcuts to silently toggle
Do Not Disturb. Create them in Shortcuts app:

1
Hush On New Shortcut → Set Focus → Do Not Disturb → Turn On
Rename shortcut to Hush On
2
Hush Off New Shortcut → Set Focus → Do Not Disturb → Turn Off
Rename shortcut to Hush Off
3
Silence Shortcuts Banners System Settings → Notifications → Shortcuts
Turn off Allow Notifications

You're all set!

Click the bell in your menu bar to toggle DND.
